Account Deletion

Last updated: August 10, 2025

You can delete your Phytoplankton Air Systems account and all associated data at any time.

In-App Method: Open the Navigation drawer (☰) in the app, then select Delete Account. Follow the prompts to confirm.

Request via Email

If you can’t access the app, send an email to algaerithms@gmail.com with the subject “Account Deletion Request” and include the email address linked to your account. We’ll process your request within next 72 hours.

What Happens Next

  • Your account and all personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • This includes profile information, preferences, and stored sensor history.
  • Deletion is irreversible—once removed, your data cannot be recovered.
