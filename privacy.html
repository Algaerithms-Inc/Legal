Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

Phytoplankton Air Systems values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data: name, email, phone number
  • App preferences: dark mode, screen lock, accessibility filters
  • Sensor data: CO₂ levels, light exposure, algae status, water level
  • Diagnostic data: crash logs and usage (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To send notifications about system status and alerts
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To fix bugs and improve performance

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on your device and, where necessary, in Firebase (Google) for authentication, cloud backup, and diagnostics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data stored in Firebase is used only for app-related functions.

5. Your Choices

You can update your profile or delete your account in the app at any time. Deleting your account removes all stored personal data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at algaerithms@gmail.com.
