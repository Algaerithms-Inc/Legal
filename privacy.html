Privacy Policy

Phytoplankton Air Systems values your privacy. This policy describes the types of data we collect, how it's used, and how it's stored.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data: name, birthdate, email, phone number
  • App preferences: dark mode, screen lock, accessibility filters
  • Sensor data: CO₂ levels, light exposure, algae status, water level
  • Diagnostic data: crash logs and usage (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve app features
  • To notify you of air system conditions and alerts
  • To retain your personalized settings and usage context
  • To monitor performance and resolve technical issues

3. Data Storage and Processing

  • Data is stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms
  • Some features use Firebase (by Google) for secure authentication, cloud storage, and diagnostics

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third-party companies. Data stored through Firebase is used strictly for app-related functionality.

5. User Control

You can manage your profile and preferences within the app. You may delete all stored data using the Navigation drawer (hamburger icon) → Delete Account feature in Settings, or submit a request via our Account Deletion page.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. All changes will be reflected within the app for your review.
