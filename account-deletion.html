Account Deletion

Last updated: August 10, 2025

You can delete your Phytoplankton Air Systems account and all associated data at any time.

In-App Method: Open the Navigation drawer (☰) in the app, then select Delete Account and follow the prompts.

Request via Online Form (no login required)

Fill out this form and we’ll confirm the request by email. We aim to respond within 72 hours.

By submitting, you authorize us to verify ownership of the email and process your deletion request.

What Happens Next

  • We verify your request via the email you provided.
  • Once verified, we permanently remove your account and associated personal data (profile info, preferences, sensor history).
  • Deletion is irreversible — once removed, your data cannot be recovered.

For more details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
